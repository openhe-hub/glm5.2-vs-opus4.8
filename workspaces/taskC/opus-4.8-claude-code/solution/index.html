03 — Principles

Four decisions, held firmly.

A short list, because each one earned its place by being argued over for months.

01

One uninterrupted strand

A 420 mm edge-lit module runs the full length of the arm — no dots, no hotspots, no diodes peeking through. The desk reads one clean line of light, not a row of points.

02

Dim the way daylight does

A capacitive ribbon along the arm slides output from a 4000 K working white down to a 2700 K amber. Brightness and warmth fall together, so late evenings never feel clinical.

03

Machined, not moulded

The counterweight base is turned from a single aluminium billet, then bead-blasted and anodised. It pins the arm at any angle without a spring, a clamp, or a visible fastener.

04

Built to be opened

Driver, strand and joint are each a serviceable part with published diagrams and stocked spares. When something wears, you replace the part — not the lamp.
